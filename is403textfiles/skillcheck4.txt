This is a team skill check. Work with your teams to deploy a Node.js/Express application on AWS Elastic Beanstalk. You can continue the deployment of an application you have already, or deploy a brand new application. The requirements:

    Application deployed on Elastic Beanstalk (30 points)
    Include a custom DNS record (can be an is404.net subdomain) to point to your application (10 points)
    The application must work with an RDS database (5 points)
    The application must include an HTTPS certificate (5 points)

To submit, create a document containing the following:

    Screenshots demonstrating your completion of the requirements.
